Director(s): Rajkumar Hirani

Cast: Ranbir Kapoor (as Sanjay Dutt), Sonam Kapoor (as Ruby), Paresh Rawal (as Sunil Dutt), Vicky Kaushal (as Paresh Ghelani), Manisha Koirala (as Nargis Dutt)

Genre(s): Drama, Biography

Spoken Languages: Hindi   

Storyline: Few lives in our times are as dramatic and enigmatic as the saga of Sanjay Dutt. Coming from a family of cinema legends, he himself became a film star, and then saw dizzying heights and darkest depths: adulation of diehard fans, unending battles with various addictions, brushes with the underworld, prison terms, loss of loved ones, and the haunting speculation that he might or might not be a terrorist. Sanju is in turns a hilarious and heartbreaking exploration of one man’s battle against his own wild self and the formidable external forces trying to crush him. It depicts the journey of a man through everything that life can throw at him. Some true stories leave you thinking “did this really happen?” This is one such unbelievable story that happens to be true.
